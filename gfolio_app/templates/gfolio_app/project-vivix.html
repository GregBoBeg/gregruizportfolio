ViviXTV.com is a website for a company I was launching before Covid drastically impacted the hospitality industry and our international supply chain. Although I have decided to not pursue the business, the website still serves as a good example of basic website design for small businesses.

The site needed to be a basic-brochure style site with no programmable functionality; therefore, it was built on the Weebly platform, using a template that I customized for our needs. The site incorporates our logo design, market messaging, downloadable product specs, and actual photos of our products and manufacturing facilities.

This site is a good example of my design work, if you are looking for a simple brochure-style website, tailored around your products, services, and marketing needs.
