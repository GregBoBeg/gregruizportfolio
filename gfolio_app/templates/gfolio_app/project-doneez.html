Application Overview

DoneEZ.com is a platform that brings a comunity of Auto Mechanics and Parts Suppliers together to connect and conduct buisiness. Parts Suppliers make auto-repair parts available to members of the platform at a special, proprietary discount. Auto Mechanics can search for nearby Parts Suppliers and receive quicker turnaround times for the vehicles they are currently servicing.

Behind The Scenes

DoneEZ was built in Django on a PostgreSQL database. It leverages the strength of Django’s Admin Console, Security capabilities and a Custom User model to create division between different types of businesses that make up the platform’s community.

The application includes a unique custom registration process that locks the user into a multi-step registration proces, and for certain business types, ending with an Admin review and approval process.

It also uses geo-mapping to search businesses, plot them on an OpenStreetMap representation, and calculate distances between suppliers.

The application architecture is structured to grow the platform’s community with a variety of business types, each with customizable functionality and product offerings.

The AWS platform includes advanced configurations for Elastic Beanstalk, Amazon RDS for PostgreSQL, Amazon Route53, Elastic Load Balancing, AWS Secrets Manager, AWS Identity & Access Management, and S3 storage buckets.
