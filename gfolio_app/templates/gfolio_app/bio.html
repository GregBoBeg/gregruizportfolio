Greg Ruiz

I grew up in New Orleans, with a love for its great music and food. While in New Orleans, I developed career strengths in business and technology through my education and work experience. The beautiful and historic St. Charles Avenue is where I obtained a BS in Computer Information Systems and an MBA from Tulane University.

In 2006, a year after Hurrican Katrina swamped the Crescent City, I relocated to Orlando with my wife and 3 children.

Today, I describe myself as a corporate-entrepreneur, reflecting my professional corporate experience and my thrive for tackling tough technology startups. I have 8 years of web development experience, as well as an extensive project management background for both software and business projects.

During my free time, I enjoy reconnecting with the blues of New Orleans through occasional jam sessions on my guitar and drums. Like software, music gives me a sense of "creativity".
